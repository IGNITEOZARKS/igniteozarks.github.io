We Exist to Help Young Adults!

The Young Adults Ministry serves those between ages 19-39 who are looking for purpose, community, and discipleship. We are a diverse group pursuing the life God designed for us, and whether you are single, married, working, in college, etc., there is a place for you here!

Connecting Young Adults with Jesus Christ

The Young Adults Ministry fosters personal and communal growth of young adults toward a relationship with Jesus Christ leading to Christian maturity.

Connecting Young Adults in a Safe Place

The Young Adults Ministry exists to create a safe place to encourage young adults (19-39) to grow in a relationship with Jesus and be part of His mission to change the world.
