GROWTH TRACK

Growth Track happens every month on first three consecutive Sundays

Growth Track is designed to help you discover your purpose and live the life God created for you.

Growth Track meets for about an hour each week after worship. Childcare WILL BE provided!

Growth Track is made up of four steps that equip you to:

  • Step 1
    Connect to IGNITE church
  • Step 2
    Discover     your strengths and purpose
  • Step 3
    Develop     your personal leadership
  • Step 4
    Use your God-given gifts to Make a Difference in the lives of others
