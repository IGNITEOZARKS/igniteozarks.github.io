NEXT STEPS

Next Steps is for folks who have attended IGNITE Church for a week to 4 years!

Next Steps is designed to help you discover your purpose and live the life God created for you.

Next Steps  meets for about an hour before worship. 

Next Steps  is made up of three steps that equip you to:

  • Step 1
    Connect to IGNITE church
  • Step 2
    Discover     your strengths and purpose
  • Step 3
    Develop and use your God-given gifts to make a difference in the lives of others
